Cong Ma

 

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at UC Berkeley, advised by Professor Martin Wainwright.

Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. at Princeton University under the supervision of Professor Yuxin Chen and Professor Jianqing Fan. I have also worked with Professor Han Liu. Prior to attending Princeton University, I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2015.

I am broadly interested in mathematics of data science, machine learning, high-dimensional statistics, convex and nonconvex optimization as well as their applications to neuroscience.

I'll be joining the Department of Statistics at the University of Chicago in July 2021.
